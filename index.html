About Us

Circle Of Sound is an unofficial record label that focuses primarily on hardcore and metal music. We do not have any contracts or obligations. All we require is for you to contact us and ask if we would be willing to feature some of your content. Even though hardcore and metal are preferred, we accept many other genres as long as you are a small band/artist. If you ever decide you want to leave, just tell us and you're free to go. Even after you leave we can still keep your music up to promote it if you'd like.
