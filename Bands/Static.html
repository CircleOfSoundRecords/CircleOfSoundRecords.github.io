Necrotic Desires


Status: Active
Formed: 2023

About:
Necrotic Desires, previously named Static, was the artist's first attempt at making any sort of music. After making 2 demos, Necrotic Desires was on a somewhat-hiatus, making unreleased music occasionally. Necrotic Desires came back in April 2024 and made 2 releases, Northern Michigan Grindcore and United States of AIPAC.
