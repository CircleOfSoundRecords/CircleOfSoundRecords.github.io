Clairvoyant Disease


Status: Active
Formed: 2024

About:
Solo project from Onaway, Michigan. Formed in 2024 after the person who made Static decided to change genres. Butterfly Effect is the first release and contains the first songs ever made by Clairvoyant Disease. Brain Hemorrhage, made as a joke, is a 22 song album that is less than 1 minute long.
